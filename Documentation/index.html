OpenXP

An open source 2D top-down RPG editor and engine

Welcome to OpenXP!

Current version: v0.0.00a

OpenXP is a 2d RPG game engine creation suite, which aims to support and extend the capabilities of your existing RPG Maker XP projects. OpenXP is free to anyone who owns the original RPG Maker XP, and open source to unlock the full potential of your creativity, in the event our enhancements aren't quite enough.
